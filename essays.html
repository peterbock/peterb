The Full Tilt Boogie

I was in a wheelchair with bike pedals
others were in motorized chairs
laughing at me or hurling insults.

We raced past Disneyland in Anaheim
into a huge 8 story blue marble building
chock full of people with missing or
malfunctioning deformed limbs.

I went as fast as I could through all
the floors, all the very large expansive rooms filled
with all races of people
receiving all kinds of help
"Patients" outnumbered the "help" by 1,000:1
There were hundreds of thousands of people crammed everywhere in this building.

An armless Indian girl slipped off an operating table
into my arms and smiled as she slid
out of my lap onto the floor to squirm.

An African woman also without arms, missing half a leg
had a really cool shoe on her one useless foot with a key in the midsole
I put the key in deeper and turned it
she tilted her head and eyes and said "Thank you!"

By now my wheelchair was gone
I was jogging through the floors
Touching and hugging thousands
of "damaged" people
I could feel the energy go right out of me
and then I'd feel it fill me up
proportionate to the degree of need of the next person
I randomly encountered.

Each floor, each time through as fast as I could manage
touching, hugging, grasping, touching
The energy released and filled again effortlessly
until after scores of times
through it became
filled with joyous, ecstatic people.

It was the Full Tilt Boogie.

Thoughts upon waking: I embodied Hafiz, Jesus, and Zachariah Kral. This should be no surprise to people who know Zach's penchant for dancing. A Full Tilt Boogie is a mass of people who wake up to their divinity which transcends their physical forms. The only response to this awakening is to dance, dance, dance!

Greater works are we to do. Jesus raised the dead, cured the lame, healed people left and right. Greater works are we to do just because. It's not a matter of "have you achieved that level of faith to do greater works?" No. It's a matter of will you just start assuming that greater works is the norm? Will you start shedding the weight of errant thinking that weighs you down from ever walking on water with grace? People need YOU!

Yes, YOU! Your flaws don't matter a nano-iota in the grand realm of this eternal moment. Do not judge your Self anymore. Do not question why God made you this way or that. Just be you, unfettered from negative false thinking, free of the illusions that seduce you, take you off the straight and narrow that leads to a full vibrant life without restrictions.

The Full Tilt Boogie in other words

Ready, Aim, Love

We're all in this together. No man is an island. No man or woman is separate from others. As one person suffers, we all suffer whether we can perceive this or not, whether we feel it with our bodies or have shut it down.

Elevating the individual above the common diminishes that individual and the common, and vice versa. It's important to recognize the individual attributes that are given, to acknowledge the effort, energy and time put into what the individual have given to make the common a better place.

Those who advocate weapons or the use of weapons do not have their own interests nor the fellow humans' best interest at heart. Since my friend will not answer my questions about how one can reconcile being "open to life" and yet advocate the use of weapons, I have to conclude that at a minimum he can't answer the questions.

History has shown that there is no shortage of people who will defend the indefensible. Those who advocate the use of weapons tend to die by the use of weapons, and tend to deceive themselves and others that the right to bear arms was meant to defend themselves against their neighbors.

The conceit and deceit lies in the overarching fantasy that with weapons we can confront and rid the commons of violent people. But history has shown that violence begets violence begets violence. We reap what we sow: if you're willing tom put bullets into another body, how can you escape bullets being put into your own?

We each choose to pick up a gun and aim it at what we perceive to be our problem, or we make love our aim. Love is not a feeling. Love is the recognition that we are in this together. Love is the willingness to hold other people accountable to make this world a better place. Love does not use a weapon when there are disagreements. Love doesn't shut down communications. Love is open to debate and is willing to reach a consensus that is best for everyone.

Some call this socialism, others call it communism. I call it community.

Power of Belief

Never underestimate the power of belief. It doesn't matter if you believe rightly or wrongly, believe in heaven and/or hell. What matters is you believe - your thoughts and actions always follow your beliefs.

This issue becomes acute when you're attempting to change any aspect of your life. In order to change you need to accurately identify the limiting belief that no longer serves you.

Ego is the repository of your belief system. Your mind, how you think. What you say reflects what you believe. These reinforce your belief system.

The political arena is a titanic clash of 3 powerful belief systems. Obama believes in your innate goodness. Romney believe in money. And the propaganda machine known as "the media" believes in omission of details so as to always give itself wiggle room under the cover of "objectivity".

The media exploits your belief system, which is primarily a conservative point of view. 80% of all content is written by PR firms. I assume the other 20% is written by sports journalists because it doesn't require much specialization. It's all opinion anyways save for "reporting" on scores which is what computers do automatically.

Into this virtual world explodes belief systems. If you're watching ANY amount of TV you're allowing your belief systems and consequential thoughts to be manipulated by corporations.

That's not my belief by the way. That's my experience because I do not watch TV. I dare you to try it: unplug your TV and put it in a closet for 1 month. Or stick it in the garage.

Then get busy doing all the things you want to do. Read books. Learn to cook new vegetarian recipes. Spent 30 minutes doing yoga for exercise. Reboot into a new you.

Millenials

Millenials: A Generation Delayed

Millenials graduated from an educational system that needs to reboot itself for contraction. The contraction will happen whether we want it to or not. The use of pesticides will contract because they're not sustainable. Farmers are right to be skeptical of new/old herbicides - they know they're going to run into resistance issues - again.

Likewise the Consumer society of the past 50+ years is contracting. It has a very tenuous hold on Millenials. If they would just ditch their TVs I believe they can reinvent society to focus on the most needful things, such as.... food, a human's numero uno need.

  • Got shelter? Check
  • Got clothing? Check
  • Got food to go with shelter and clothing? If not, then it cancels out the need for shelter and clothing.

 

The Farm Bill needs to phase out subsidies to methods that don't work. While it's unfortunate that chemical farmers have become dependent on Federal subsidies via the Farm Bill, the sooner we subsidize organic farming, the better off we'll be.

And we'll have to start transitioning to organic anyways because the #ChemAg "revolution" started in 1971 has crippled the environment and compromised planetary health.

Legalism Is Regressive

Render to Caesar what belongs to Caesar; render to God what belongs to God. Political/legal/religious solutions are anything but. There's always someone trying to find a way out from whatever they feel like isn't fair to them. Spiritual materialism thinks God has blessed them because of their thinking, or beliefs, and never their actions.

You shall know them by their fruits. But most people today vote based on what someone says, not on their fruits. I say all the right things but my walk fails to match my words, and you'll just chalk it up to being human. I suffer no consequence. Emboldened by your so-called 'turning the other cheek' I widen the scope of my malfeasance which gains me more money. You'll forgive me because that's what Christ compels you to do.

Because I'm getting away with my malfeasance which harms you, I continue to exploit your beliefs for my own personal gain, and in the core of my essence I'm a wolf in sheep's clothing and you whom I'm exploiting are deaf, dumb, blind and born to follow. You're so willing to believe that I follow after God's heart - after all God is blessing me by allowing my thievery to continue unabated, without consequence, without a hint of justice in these lawless times - that you'll rationalize away any hint of variation to being human. You'll never figure it out just because I say so.

Make no mistake: everyone, absolutely everyone on this planet is currently engaged in one of two types of behavior: giving or stealing. From that derives all other kinds of positive or negative behavior. Where to draw the line?

The above describes legalism which is adherence to the letter of the law. Legalism can quickly become legislated lawlessness, which is exactly the social and political environment we find ourselves in today.

What of the spirit of the law? You know, love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, goodness, gentleness, faithfulness, and self-control; against such there are no restrictions. No belief system required. No attendance at church required. No prayer, no subscriptions, no requirements whatsoever will prevent you from manifesting the fruits of the spirit.

Do you see the contrast now between the 10 commandments and the 9 fruits of the spirit? All law (legalism) has "no" (restrictions) at its core. The fruits of the spirit have none. They are all "yes" and positive to both you and your neighbors.

All law is based on the past, not today. All love can happen only in this moment. Only this moment is available for you to leave the restrictions of legalism and choose love.

This is why it's written "Love your neighbor as yourself". Not your fellow church-goer, but that asshole next door. Jesus in all of his distressing disguises.

Love: how you treat those outside your circle/class

There is no greater love than this: that a man should lay down his life for his friends. (John 16:13)

As you have done it to the least of these, you've done it also to me. (Matt. 25:40)

These words were spoken not to believers, but to the very first followers of Jesus. Like us, they struggled to understand what love was. Jesus makes it clear: whatever you do to others you are doing to him. This is unconditional love.

There are no preconditions required to love someone. They need not be a believer, go to church, practice the same brand of religion, nor speak the same language as you. They only requirement is no requirements whatsoever.

How then is it that the love of many has grown cold? How come many have allowed themselves to be blinded by the rigid dogmas of their ignorance and hate? How come anyone would allow themselves to be manipulated by money when Jesus was clear: you cannot serve God and mammon (money)?

Yet here we are. People fervently support a wealthy man who needs no additional money. They "believe" in him for no other reason than they too serve money. Jesus said "render to Caesar what belongs to Caesar, and render to God what belongs to God" and yet they give this man their money.

Jesus said keep 1 coat, 1 pair of sandals. Keep it light. Don't go heavy in materialism because it will weigh you down. Keep it simple. How many of you have things? How many of those things actually own you?

You can't take it with you, so why not liberate yourself of them now? Give them to someone in need.
