OrgAnnex

Organic agriculture (organiculture) can do far more than feed the world:

  • Clean the land. Let's get rid of 80,000+ synthetic toxic chemicals and let Nature work her magic
  • Clean the water. What goes into the land comes back to it via rain, rivers and aquafiers.
  • Clean the atmosphere. It's not just carbon being whipped into the air- it's also toxic chemicals

Note

The land-water-atmosphere are inseparable. Synthetic chemicals destroy the soil organisms that give the soil its fertility. Restoring soil fertility cleans the land, water and atmosphere. Any time you eat processed food you are destroying America's #1 asset and the highest life form on Earth: the soil.
