Organic

My life-course altering sickness ended yesterday after finally being able to overcome my sickness's end, which it knew to be when I consumed raw garlic. I knew that garlic is the answer in most cases but it has to be done at the right time or it will be rejected by the body. Garlic and a hot bath did the trick. 

My desire for the usual foods is gone. In its place is a desire to walk through my humble greenhouse and pick the fresh leaves of spinach, cilantro and various lettuce, which I do 3-4 times a day. If I were to pack it down it would amount to less than 1/4 cup per visit. Not much volume-wise, but it's rich in nutrients.

While grazing on the cilantro I was reminded of it's heavy metal chelation properties. For those of your who don't know, heavy metals eventually reside in your gut and block absorption of nutrients. Cilantro is one of the few remediators that do a swell job of removing this nutrient blocking madness.

I say madness because a poor diet fucks with your mind, your thinking, which you might know resides in your gut - and why wouldn't it? Entire books could be written about how a properly nourished human has intestinal flora nearly identical to locale the food originated.

This is where organic soil comes into clear focus. In the very least, eat organic veggies and fruits. But for optimal health grow what you want to it. I will always be dependent in a good way on local farmers to grow food that is organic so that I can eat the freshest food possible. And nothing is fresher than picking it and eating it on the spot.

When it's done this way, it has to be organic or I guarantee you you will never grow a single thing, let alone eat what you grow because you can always drive a few blocks and eat organic veggies that most likely were shipped a few days ago from California. So, if you grow your own, try this challenge: grow lettuce or kale, pick it, store it for 7 days, and then eat it. Your body will tell you right away that this is pretty bland fare. Why would you grow food and not pick and eat?

When you do pick and eat, you begin to change and you'll also notice something else if you stick with daily picking: a connection of some sort, and I'll tell you right now what that connection is but unless you do it you will not believe me.

The connection to the earth, to the soil, is not merely physical, but in fact metaphysical. The way I use this word is not the way the standard obscuratory dictionary uses the word, which points to unclarity, meaningless, mystical, unknowing. In this day of conscious raising energizing clarity and purposefully positive intent as practiced and communicated by Zachariah Kral, metaphysical means "beyond physical, per se, a physical connection with Source."

Our metaphysical connection begins with the soil organisms that create the basis of all life. Without those life-giving organisms, there would be no plants. Without plants, there could not be animals, with out support for animals, there could not be humans. This does not mean humans should consume animals, or are dependent on them for sustenance. It means that animals can be consumed secondarily, or tertiary.

It also means that our source is the soil. When you consume genetically engineered organisms, you are allowing an artificial connection into your bodies' realm of internal communications. The way you think, act, communicate with your self and others, every moment depends on what you ingest.

Hyperbole? Not at all because the facts are that anything you ingest modifies your body's communication system: the endocrine glands, which communicate with each other to keep your body functioning, for better or worse.

For me to continue to grow as a human and participate in this incredible shift of consciousness, this shedding of old belief systems of my ego's wineskin so that I am the new wine dispensed liberally, freely so all of our cups overflow into greater overspillings of love, joy, peace, I can no longer justify consuming that which eventually will make me sick and less useful. I can no longer participate in the same old same old which merely hopes to manifest something better.

No, the plant, the beliefs I've allowed into my gut must be pulled out by the roots and new plants that I know will manifest the desired fruits of health for self and all are planted in its place. As of today. As of now.

I cannot change the labeling laws of the land. Our solutions are not political and never were. That's mere illusion, brass when it's pure gold that I want and that gold is within me.

I cannot be against something. I must be for something and what I'm for is Organic, for everyone to start to grow something somewhere and eat it each day. A container in a window, a container outside, a small patch of garden. Kill a patch of grass. Garden year round. If it grows in USDA Zone 3 in Maine in the dead of winter then by Gods and Goddesses - YOU! - what's your excuse for not growing something you can pick and eat that will be for your betterment?

Do not be fooled. You can grow just about anything anytime of the year. You just need to know how and follow the practices. 

The above is one of two ways that I can love each of you reading this. Keep growing, keep spiraling out into ever greater orbits of consciousness and manifesting intent.

~ Peter Bockenthien
